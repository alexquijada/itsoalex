>Hello There

Accessibility focused product designer

I create unique user journeys while adhering to industry standards and best practices to ensure a stress-free and familiar experience.

I make sure that users are able to properly locate the purchase button, find where to go to next after reading a story on a page, make sure that they are able to notice important call-to-actions on a page and develop a site with the visitor in mind. I do all of this by using user psychology, gestalt principles, and UX research. Check me out in action presenting on these best practices here: UX Best Practices Seminar

I have leveraged my background in the architectural industry along with my graphic design knowledge to help develop my user-first approach to user experience design.
