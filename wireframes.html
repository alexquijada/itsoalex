Destination Marketing Organizations (DMO) Strategy Intro

Whenever developing wireframes for a client, I review their SEO statistics, any CRO recommendations, heatmaps if available, and the google analytics data.

The process involves using the clients' google analytics information to create the Information Architecture and SEO strategy.
This information also helps to narrow down their key target audience and we evalaute against what demographic the client wants to target.
We also integrate the client's KPIs into the strategy and helps to drive the design process.

All of this information helped establish a strategy to develop their information architecture, personas, the wireframes, and the ulitmately their entire websites' design.

Wireframe Strategy

The client will usually define what their KPIs are for their website design and will also inform us of their marketing goals. We then take these goals and measure them up against the SEO, CRO, any heatmaps, user testing, and google analytics data to create the wireframes and user journey.


  • I design the wireframes with desktop and mobile best practices, including but not limited to:
  • • Web Accessibility Standards
  • • Proven best practices by world leading organizations such as Nielsen Norman & Rosenfeld Enterprise
  • • Gestalt Pricinples
  • • Interaction Design Principles
